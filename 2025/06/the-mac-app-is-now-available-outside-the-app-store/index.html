The Mac app is now available outside the App Store

Jun 2, 2025

Tags: announcements app-store mac

Today, I’m thrilled to announce that the Mac app can be finally downloaded without the App Store, and soon even via the Homebrew Cask. It took serious work for it to take off, but after a few weeks of daily usage it seems to behave as expected.

There are still limitations in that the app, for now, doesn’t support payments. This means that paid features are not available (yet), but they will be introduced at a later time:

  • Apple TV
  • iCloud
  • Network settings
  • On-demand
  • OTP
  • Providers

You can read a side story about the development of this new, standalone Mac app on my website.

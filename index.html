Passepartout

Your go-to app for VPN and privacy.

  • OpenVPN (plus XOR patch)
  • WireGuard
  • On-demand rules
  • Override DNS and HTTP proxy
  • Custom routing
  • Presets for multiple providers (virtually any)

Tailored for Apple platforms:

  • Apple TV
  • iCloud
  • Shortcuts
  • No background activities
  • Wise on battery

Download

App Store

Standalone for Mac

Passepartout is available for Mac outside the App Store on GitHub Releases, though currently limited to free features only:

  • Passepartout for Mac (Apple Silicon) (GPG)
  • Passepartout for Mac (Intel) (GPG)

It's recommended that you verify the GPG signatures with my public key.

You may also get Passepartout with Homebrew:

brew install passepartout
